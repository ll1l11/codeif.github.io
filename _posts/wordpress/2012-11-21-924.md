<http://www.pythonware.com/products/pil/>

<http://stackoverflow.com/questions/2088304/installing-pil-python-imaging-library-in-win7-64-bits-python-2-6-4>

<http://www.lfd.uci.edu/~gohlke/pythonlibs/>
